

System: Liverpool Uni Building management card access system
Students and staff have a card for card access to specific buildings.
Not all students could enter all buildings.
Not all staff could enter all buildings.
Special access required to veterinary, labs etc.
Cards are issued at start of year, students allocated a student number, staff a staff number.
Cards may be replaced if lost by student or joined any time of year by staff.
A card has a number, photo, barcode. (COMPOSITIONAL ASSOC.)
